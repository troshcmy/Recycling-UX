About A2Z Recyclers

At A2Z Recyclers, we're dedicated to making our planet greener, one recyclable at a time. Join us in our mission to reduce pollution, conserve natural resources, and combat climate change through recycling.

Discover the benefits of recycling:

  • Reduced Waste: Less garbage in landfills and incinerators.
  • Pollution Prevention: Making a cleaner environment.
  • Resource Conservation: Preserving our natural resources.

  • Energy Savings: Reducing greenhouse gas emissions.

  • Job Creation: Contributing to economic growth.

CLOSING THE LOOP ON WASTE

A2Z Recycling conduct waste management from collection and transport to the treatment of materials, with the aim to produce new products from old resources and return them to the market. The core purpose at A2Z Recycling is to create a circular economy by closing the loop on waste whilst offering the benefits of cost savings to customers.

LANDFILL DIVERSION AND RESOURCE RECOVERY

Commercial and Industrial (C&I) waste makes up a third (20.4 million tonnes) of total waste generated in Australia, with 58% of the waste being recycled1. When you opt for commercial waste disposal through reDirect Recycling, you are assisting in creating sustainable manufacturing processes by closing the loop on waste and contributing towards the circular economy.
